Actors Centre Australia (ACA) is a private, world-class dramatic arts training facility dedicated to producing Australia's most sought after actors as well as surturing creative development through our public programs.

ACA was founded by Dean Carey in 1987, and as Creative Director he continues to ensure all of the courses are balanced with a mixture of modern skills along with time-honoured performance technique.

This vibrant creative arts centre offers Full-time and Part-time Courses, Master Classes, Short courses in Acting, Drama, Musical Theatre, Film Studies, Television, Presenting, Singing, Voice Overs, Screen Skills and On Screen Training and Corporate Development.

In addition to the various courses, ACA offers a range of other specialised training, coaching and support products including the online acting course ‘The Acting Edge’, books, manuals and audition apps to ensure you are totally prepared when heading into any casting.

All courses have been designed to introduce, develop and strengthen the individual actor, whether first igniting the passion of performance and creative vibrancy, right through to a seasoned professional’s ongoing growth.
