part-time acting course


This is Australia's premier part-time acting course, launched in 1990 and is dedicated to activating, nurturing and celebrating your creativity. This course has been constructed to offer you the best and most comprehensive part-time acting training possible. It is a dynamic and flexible program that provokes, nurtures and celebrates your development. the part-time course offers from one and up to eight modules that complement each other. Combined, these modules offer you the most flexible and comprehensive part-time acting and performance experiences possible.

"On a creative and professional level the Part-Time Course will develop your performance skills in a way that celebrates, supports and challenges you in an environment that I have not found in any other place I have taught. I am proud to act as spokesperson for this exciting program and promise you incredible learning opportunities as you grow as an actor and creative individual."Andrew Lloyde - Founding Director, Part-Time Course
“Actors Centre is one of Australia’s premier arts training institutions and it relishes its role in fostering new artists. Offering a diverse and comprehensive range of courses, the Part-time Course community is a hotbed of development and creativity. Students from a wide range of experiences and cultures are tutored by seasoned and established actors and teachers, collaborating to establish a challenging yet immensely enjoyable forum for both art and artist to flourish. It is a rewarding and gratifying opportunity to be involved in, and one that I find constantly surprising and inspiring. The growth and improvement in skill, confidence and sense of play in our students is always something to behold."Johann Walraven - Part-Time Course Co-ordinator
